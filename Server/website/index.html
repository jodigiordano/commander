What is Commander ?
Commander is the most awesome real-time strategy / tower defense on planet Earth with innovative game mechanics, a gorgeous art direction and a memorable "easy to learn, hard to master" gameplay experience.




You are the rising leader of the resistance against a mysterious alien lifeform that is trying to anihilate the humanity (!)

Bend the path taken by the asteroids with a special gravitational turret!
Hi, my name is Jodi Giordano, aka j0d1, and I run Ephemere Games, which is a micro game company located in Montreal, Quebec. Commander is my first game as an independent developer. I'm giving a shot at the indie life for one year now and I love it!
